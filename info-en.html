Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do you offer catering services?
Yes, we offer catering for events and private parties. Please contact us directly for more information and a customized quote.

Do you have special menu options for allergies or specific diets?
Yes, we offer gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan options. Please inform our staff of your specific needs when placing your order.

Can I order takeout from your bistro?
Yes, we offer a to-go option for many of our dishes. Orders can be placed by phone or in person.

How far in advance should I make a reservation for large groups?
For groups of 10 or more, we recommend making a reservation at least two weeks in advance to ensure we can accommodate your needs.

Do you accept gift vouchers?
No, we do not offer gift vouchers that can be purchased at our bistro.

How can I get updates on upcoming events and special offers?
We keep you updated through our newsletter and social media channels. Follow us on Facebook and Instagram to stay informed!

Is there parking available nearby?
Yes, there are both street parking and a parking lot on the school grounds, right next to the bistro.

What are your safety and hygiene measures?
We take the safety of our guests very seriously. Our team follows strict hygiene protocols, and we ensure that all surfaces are regularly disinfected.
