Our Offer

Our range includes freshly made sandwiches, crisp salads, hot meals, and delicious snacks and beverages. We place great importance on regional and seasonal ingredients to always provide the best quality to our students and staff. Vegetarian and vegan options are a given for us, ensuring there's something for every taste and dietary preference.

Our Team

Our friendly and dedicated team consists of experienced kitchen staff who ensure the well-being of our school community daily with passion and creativity. They are supported by students who gain valuable insights into gastronomy through projects or internships.

Sustainability and Responsibility

We are committed to sustainability and environmental awareness. That's why we use biodegradable packaging and aim to minimize food waste. Additionally, we work closely with local suppliers to ensure short transport routes and fresh products.

Community and Interaction

The school bistro is more than just a place to eat. It is a space for interaction and exchange, where friendships are made and breaks are enjoyed together. With cozy seating and an inviting atmosphere, we warmly welcome all members of our school community to feel comfortable with us.
