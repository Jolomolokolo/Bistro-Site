The Bistro:

Our bistro is the perfect place for a relaxing break, offering a variety of freshly prepared meals and drinks. We prioritize quality and regional ingredients, providing both vegetarian and allergy-friendly options so that everyone can find something suitable and feel completely at ease.

Our Offer:

Our offer includes a wide range of food and beverages specifically tailored to the needs of teachers and students. We provide a selection of sandwiches, salads, sweets, and drinks, all of which are fresh and delicious. Quality and freshness are our top priorities. Our dishes are suitable for both teachers and students, including vegetarian options. For those with allergies, we offer suitable alternatives, including lactose-free, gluten-free, and nut-free foods. We also have appropriate dishes for people with fructose or histamine intolerance. This way, we ensure that everyone, whether teacher or student, can find something tasty and suitable with us.
